Holiday Only Mashed Potatoes

These mashed potatoes are the perfect side dish for any holiday meal. They're creamy, fluffy, and loaded with flavor. They're made with Yukon Gold potatoes, which are naturally creamy and buttery, and they're seasoned with garlic, onion, and fresh herbs. They're the perfect side dish for Thanksgiving, Christmas, Easter, or any other holiday meal.

Ingredients

  • Yukon Gold Potatoes
  • Butter
  • Heavy Cream
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Chives
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the potatoes.
  2. Mash the potatoes.
  3. Add the butter, cream, garlic, onion, chives, salt, and pepper.
